Instructions

Log into your D&D Beyond Account.
Move your mouse over the 'Characters' tab at the top of the window and select 'My Characters'.
Find the character you want to convert and click 'View'.
Take a look at the Address bar, it should look something like:
https://www.dndbeyond.com/profile/YourUserName/characters/8675309

Find the Character ID, in this case is 8675309.
Paste this ID in the 'Character ID' text box and click 'Get Character Data'.

It will take a few seconds or more (30?) in order for the data to populate.
This all depends on how fast your computer is, as it's pasting a whole lot of data.

Click the 'Download XML' button and the data that's in the lower box will be saved to your computer.

This file is the one that you want to import into Fantasy Grounds.
